Privacy
Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-24  ·  Applies to: Stims for Android

Short version: Stims collects no personal data, sends nothing to any server, and contains no third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising. Everything the app does stays entirely on your device.

§ 01

Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. Stims does not ask for, store, or transmit any data that identifies you or your device to us or to any third party.

§ 02

Permissions Used

Stims requires one special permission to function:

  • Usage Access — allows the app to detect which app is currently in the foreground. This is used exclusively to determine whether to acquire or release a screen wake lock. The data is read in-process, never written to persistent storage beyond the current session, and never transmitted.

Your list of selected apps is stored in SharedPreferences — local device storage only, never synced or uploaded.

Stims has no internet permission in its manifest. It is architecturally incapable of making network requests.

§ 03

Data Sharing

We share no data with anyone because we collect no data. There are no third-party SDKs, no analytics libraries, no advertising networks, and no crash reporting services included in the app.

§ 04

Data Storage & Retention

The only persistent data Stims stores is your list of selected apps, saved to local SharedPreferences on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Can be cleared at any time via Settings → Apps → Stims → Clear data

§ 05

Children's Privacy

Stims does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected at all, the app has no age-specific data practices.

§ 06

Changes to This Policy

If the privacy practices of Stims change — for example if a future version adds a feature requiring network access — this policy will be updated and the effective date revised. The current policy always reflects the latest published version of the app.
